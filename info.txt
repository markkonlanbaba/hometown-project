## Osu Castle
Osu Castle is a castle located in Osu, Ghana on the coast of the Gulf of Guinea in Africa.


## Ghana National Museum
The National Museum of Ghana is in the Ghanaian capital, Accra. It is the largest
and oldest of the six museums under the administration of the Ghana Museums and Monuments Board.

## Gallery 1957

Gallery 1957 is a contemporary art
gallery located in Accra, Ghana. The gallery intends to present artists of West Africa and the diaspora
